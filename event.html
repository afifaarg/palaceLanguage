Spanish Language Workshop: A Day of Learning and Fun

On October 15, 2023, Palace of Language hosted an immersive Spanish Language Workshop. Here's how it went!

Interactive Learning Sessions

The workshop began with interactive sessions led by our expert instructors. Participants learned basic Spanish phrases, grammar, and pronunciation through engaging activities.

Highlights: Role-playing exercises, group discussions, and real-world conversation practice.

Exploring Spanish Culture

The event also included a deep dive into Spanish culture. Participants enjoyed traditional Spanish music, dance performances, and a tasting session of authentic Spanish cuisine.

Highlights: Flamenco dance demonstration, paella cooking showcase, and a Q&A session with native speakers.

Hands-On Language Practice

Participants engaged in hands-on activities to practice their new language skills. From writing exercises to conversational games, everyone had a chance to shine.

Highlights: Collaborative storytelling, language games, and personalized feedback from instructors.
